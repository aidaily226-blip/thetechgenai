Premium smartphones now cost over $1,000. But you absolutely do not need to spend that much to get a great phone in 2026.

Here are the best smartphones under $300 that offer excellent value.

## 1. Google Pixel 8a — $499 (often on sale for $299)

The Pixel 8a is arguably the best value smartphone on the market. It runs pure Android, gets 7 years of updates, and has a camera that rivals phones costing twice as much.

**Pros:** Outstanding camera, clean software, long software support
**Cons:** Average battery life

## 2. Samsung Galaxy A55 — $249

Samsung's A-series has gotten seriously impressive. The A55 has a beautiful AMOLED display, great performance, and Samsung's trusted brand reliability.

**Pros:** Beautiful display, solid build quality, good cameras
**Cons:** No wireless charging

## 3. OnePlus Nord N30 — $199

If you want the most phone for the least money, the Nord N30 is hard to beat. Fast charging (50W) and a smooth display at under $200 is remarkable.

**Pros:** Incredibly affordable, fast charging, smooth performance
**Cons:** Cameras are just average

## Final Verdict

For most people, the **Google Pixel 8a** (when on sale) or the **Samsung Galaxy A55** are the sweet spot of quality and price in 2026. You'll get a phone that lasts 3-4 years without any compromises in daily use.
