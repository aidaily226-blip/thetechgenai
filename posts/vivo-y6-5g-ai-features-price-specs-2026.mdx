### 1. AI Sleep Mode — Protects Your Eyes At Night

**What Vivo calls it:** AI Sleep Mode / AI Colour Temperature Tuning

**What it actually does:**

You know how your phone screen looks very blue and harsh at night? That harsh blue light messes with your brain and makes it harder to fall asleep after you put the phone down.

AI Sleep Mode on the Vivo Y6 5G fixes this automatically.

The display offers AI sleep mode for colour temperature tuning and an eye protection mode.

The AI watches what time it is. As it gets later in the evening, it slowly shifts your screen from a cool blue tone to a warmer, softer yellow-orange tone. This warmer light is much easier on your eyes and much less disruptive to your sleep.

The important word here is **AI**. A normal phone just lets you turn night mode on or off manually. The Vivo Y6 5G's AI does it automatically, gradually, and adjusts based on time of day — without you having to do anything.

**In simple words:** Your phone screen gets softer and warmer at night automatically so your eyes do not hurt and you can sleep better.

---

### 2. AI Anti-Fatigue Brightness — Adjusts Light For Your Comfort

**What it actually does:**

Most phones change brightness based on how much light is in the room. Bright room — screen gets brighter. Dark room — screen gets dimmer. Simple.

The Vivo Y6 5G goes further. Its AI does not just measure the light in the room. It also considers how long you have been looking at the screen and gradually adjusts the brightness to reduce eye strain over time.

The panel includes anti-fatigue brightness adjustment.

Think of it like this. If you stare at a bright light for one minute — fine. If you stare at the same bright light for two hours — your eyes start to hurt. The AI understands this difference and adjusts accordingly.

**In simple words:** The longer you use the phone, the smarter it gets about protecting your eyes from tiredness.

---

### 3. AI Virtual RAM — Makes Your Phone Feel Faster

**What Vivo calls it:** Extended RAM / Virtual RAM

**What it actually does:**

RAM is the space your phone uses to keep apps open in the background. More RAM means more apps can stay open at the same time without closing.

The Vivo Y6 5G has up to 12GB of real RAM. But it also has **12GB of AI Virtual RAM** on top of that — giving you up to 24GB total.

Here is how it works. The AI looks at which apps you use most and which ones you barely touch. It then uses a part of your phone's storage to act as extra RAM for the apps you actually need. This means apps load faster, switching between apps is smoother, and your phone does not slow down even when you have lots of apps open.

**In simple words:** AI borrows space from your phone's storage to act as extra memory — so your phone runs faster without needing to buy more hardware.

---

### 4. AI Camera Features — Better Photos Without Any Effort

**What it actually does:**

The Vivo Y6 5G has a 50MP main camera. That number alone does not tell you much. What matters is what the AI does with those 50 million pixels.

Here is what the AI camera on this phone does:

**Scene Detection** — Point your camera at anything — food, a sunset, a person, a building — and the AI recognises what you are photographing in less than a second. It then automatically adjusts the colour, sharpness, and brightness settings for that specific type of photo. Food photos get more vibrant colours. Landscape shots get sharper edges and richer skies. Portrait shots get softer backgrounds.

You do not press any buttons. The AI sees what you are pointing at and adjusts the camera for you.

**AI Portrait Mode** — When you take a photo of a person, the AI detects the edges of the person's body and face. It then blurs the background behind them — making the person stand out like a professional photo. What used to require an expensive camera and skill — AI does in one tap.

**Low Light Photography** — Taking photos at night or in a dark room used to mean blurry, grainy photos. The AI on the Vivo Y6 5G takes multiple photos in rapid succession and combines them — picking the sharpest parts of each — to create one clear photo even in very low light.

**AI Selfie Enhancement** — The 8MP front camera uses AI to smooth skin, adjust lighting, and even reshape the frame slightly to make selfies look more flattering — automatically, without filters.

**In simple words:** The camera figures out what you are photographing and makes the photo look good on its own. You just point and tap.

---

### 5. AI One-Click Dust and Water Removal — Cleans Your Speaker With AI

This one is genuinely clever and something most phones do not have.

**What it actually does:**

If you drop your phone in water or dust gets into the speaker grille — sound gets muffled and blocked. On most phones you have to wait for it to dry out or try to shake it clean.

The Vivo Y6 5G has a one-click AI dust and water removal feature. When you activate it, the phone uses its speakers to vibrate at a specific AI-calculated frequency that pushes water and dust out of the speaker grille.

Think of it like when Apple Watch ejects water after swimming — the Vivo Y6 5G does the same thing, but for the speaker, and it uses AI to determine the right vibration pattern.

**In simple words:** Tap one button and AI uses vibration to push water and dust out of your speaker. No more muffled sound after getting your phone wet.

---

### 6. AI Power Management — Makes The Huge Battery Last Even Longer

The Vivo Y6 5G already has a massive 7,200mAh battery that can easily last two days. But Vivo's AI makes it last even longer by managing power intelligently.

**What it actually does:**

The AI learns your habits over time. It notices that you barely use your phone between 11pm and 7am. So it slows down background processes during those hours to save battery.

It notices that you always use Instagram heavily between 6pm and 8pm. So it pre-loads Instagram into memory before 6pm so it opens faster — without wasting battery throughout the rest of the day.

It also detects when you are gaming or watching video and adjusts performance to give you maximum speed while still being efficient with battery.

**In simple words:** AI watches how you use your phone every day and learns to save battery in smart ways without making your phone feel slow.

---

### 7. AI Display — DC Dimming For Flicker-Free Screen

**What it actually does:**

Most phone screens flicker very quickly when they dim — too fast for you to see consciously. But your eyes and brain still notice it, and over time it causes headaches and eye strain.

The display offers global DC dimming for flicker-free viewing.

DC Dimming is a technology that dims the screen by actually reducing the electrical power — not by flickering. The result is a screen that your eyes find much more comfortable, especially during long use.

The AI in the Vivo Y6 5G manages this DC Dimming automatically so the screen is always as easy on your eyes as possible.

**In simple words:** The screen dims smoothly instead of flickering — so your eyes do not get tired even after hours of use.
