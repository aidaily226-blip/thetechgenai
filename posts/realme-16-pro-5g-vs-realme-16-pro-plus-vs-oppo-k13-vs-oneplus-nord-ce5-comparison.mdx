## Pros and Cons

### Realme 16 Pro 5G — ₹31,999

✅ **Pros**
- Industry leading 200MP camera with OIS
- Massive 7000mAh battery
- Beautiful bright AMOLED display
- IP65 water resistance
- Good value for camera lovers

❌ **Cons**
- Dimensity 7300 Max is weaker than competitors
- Gets warm during heavy gaming
- No wireless charging
- Realme UI has more bloatware

---

### Realme 16 Pro+ 5G — ₹39,999

✅ **Pros**
- Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 — best processor in this list
- 200MP camera with excellent daylight results
- 7000mAh battery with 80W fast charging
- Premium build quality
- IP65 water resistance

❌ **Cons**
- Most expensive of the four
- No wireless charging
- Realme UI has pre-installed apps
- Camera can struggle in very low light

---

### OPPO K13 5G — ₹17,441

✅ **Pros**
- Most affordable option by far
- 7000mAh massive battery
- 80W fast charging at this price is impressive
- IP65 water resistance
- Clean and reliable ColorOS 15

❌ **Cons**
- Weakest processor of the four
- Only 2MP depth sensor — no real ultrawide
- No 4K video recording
- Only 2 years of OS updates
- Camera performance is average

---

### OnePlus Nord CE5 5G — ₹24,999

✅ **Pros**
- Best value for money overall
- Dimensity 8350 Apex — near flagship performance
- Sony LYT-600 sensor — best low light camera
- 4 years of OS updates — best long term support
- Clean OxygenOS 16 with minimal bloatware
- 7100mAh battery

❌ **Cons**
- Only 50MP main camera vs 200MP on Realme
- Limited storage variant options
- No wireless charging
- Slightly dated design

---

## Who Should Buy Which Phone?

### Buy Realme 16 Pro 5G ₹31,999 if:
- Photography and 200MP camera is your priority
- You want massive battery life
- Budget is around ₹30,000-35,000
- You shoot lots of photos in daylight

### Buy Realme 16 Pro+ 5G ₹39,999 if:
- You want the best performance in the Realme lineup
- Gaming and heavy multitasking matters to you
- You want the most complete package available
- Budget can stretch to ₹40,000

### Buy OPPO K13 5G ₹17,441 if:
- Budget is under ₹20,000
- You need a reliable daily driver without premium features
- Battery life is more important than camera quality
- You are buying a secondary phone

### Buy OnePlus Nord CE5 5G ₹24,999 if:
- You want the best performance per rupee
- Low light photography matters to you
- Long term software support is important
- You want a clean bloatware free experience
