### Samsung Galaxy A56 — Galaxy AI Features

Samsung's AI suite is called **Galaxy AI** and it is one of the most comprehensive AI feature sets on any Android phone:

- **Circle to Search** — Draw a circle around anything on your screen and Google searches it instantly. This is exclusive to Samsung and is genuinely one of the most useful AI features on any smartphone.
- **Live Translate** — AI translates phone calls in real time during the call itself
- **Chat Assist** — AI rewrites your messages in different tones (formal, casual, polite)
- **AI Photo Edit** — Remove unwanted objects, change backgrounds, reframe images
- **Transcript Assist** — AI transcribes and summarizes voice recordings
- **Note Assist** — AI organizes and summarizes your notes automatically

### Vivo V70 — AI Features

Vivo's AI is deeply integrated with the ZEISS camera system:

- **AI Captions** — Automatically generates captions for images and videos
- **AI Erase** — Removes unwanted objects from photos intelligently
- **AI Creation** — Generates AI art and backgrounds
- **AI Magic Landscape** — Changes photo backgrounds to different seasons and locations
- **AI Noise Suppression** — Removes background noise during calls and recordings
- **Gemini Live** — Google's most advanced AI assistant built directly into the phone
- **AI Search** — Find anything on your phone by describing it in natural language

### OnePlus 13s — AI Features

OnePlus has built the most deeply integrated AI system of any Android phone with its **Plus Key** button:

- **AI Plus Mind** — A dedicated AI assistant that sorts, recalls, and organizes all your information across apps. Think of it as an AI secretary that remembers everything on your phone.
- **AI Eraser 2.0** — Most advanced object removal AI of the four phones
- **AI Call Summary** — Automatically summarizes long calls into key points
- **AI Battery Life** — Learns your usage patterns and optimizes battery automatically
- **AI Camera Editing** — Advanced photo and video editing powered by on-device AI
- **AI Frame Stabilization** — Makes shaky videos smooth automatically
- **AI Documents** — Reads, summarizes, and organizes documents automatically
- **Plus Key** — A dedicated hardware button that instantly activates any AI feature

### Apple iPhone 16 — Apple Intelligence

Apple's AI platform is called **Apple Intelligence** and is deeply integrated into iOS:

- **Writing Tools** — AI proofreads, rewrites, and summarizes text across all apps
- **Clean Up** — Removes distracting objects from photos automatically
- **Visual Intelligence** — Point camera at anything and AI tells you what it is
- **Priority Notifications** — AI decides which notifications are most important
- **Smart Reply** — AI drafts replies to messages in your writing style
- **Siri with ChatGPT** — Siri can now use ChatGPT for complex questions
- **Action Button** — Can activate AI features instantly
