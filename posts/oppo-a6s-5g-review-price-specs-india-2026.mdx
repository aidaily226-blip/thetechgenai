## Display — Good for the Price

The OPPO A6s 5G features a large 6.75-inch HD+ LCD display with a 120Hz refresh rate. At this price point, a 120Hz display is genuinely impressive — most budget phones still ship with 60Hz displays in 2026.

The 1125 nits peak brightness means you can comfortably use the phone outdoors in direct sunlight. The 240Hz touch sampling rate makes the display feel responsive and snappy during gaming and scrolling.

One limitation — this is an LCD panel, not AMOLED. Colors are not as vibrant as you would get on more expensive phones, and blacks appear grey rather than true black. For most everyday users browsing social media, watching YouTube, and messaging, this display is perfectly fine.

## Performance — Smooth for Everyday Use

The OPPO A6s 5G is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 chipset, running ColorOS 15 based on Android with AI LinkBoost 3.0, RAM Expansion, and Trinity Engine optimization for consistent performance.

The Dimensity 6300 is a capable mid-range chip for everyday tasks. You can expect smooth performance for:
- Social media apps (Instagram, WhatsApp, YouTube)
- Web browsing and streaming
- Casual gaming (BGMI on medium settings, casual titles on high)
- Multitasking between apps

Heavy games like Genshin Impact may experience frame drops on high settings, but reducing graphics quality delivers a smooth experience.

The RAM Expansion feature uses phone storage as virtual RAM — boosting effective RAM to up to 12GB — which helps keep more apps running in the background without slowdowns.

## Camera — 50MP Does the Job

The OPPO A6s 5G features a dual rear camera with a 50MP primary sensor and a 2MP monochrome sensor, offering AI Night Mode for low-light photography and Portrait Mode for depth and detail. On the front, the 16MP camera ensures high-quality selfies with AI Portrait Retouching support.

### Rear Camera Performance
- **Daylight shots** — The 50MP main camera produces detailed, sharp photos in good lighting. Colors are natural and accurate.
- **Portrait mode** — Edge detection is decent for a budget phone. Background blur looks natural in most situations.
- **Night mode** — AI Night Mode brightens low-light photos significantly. Results are usable for social media but not professional quality.
- **Video** — Records up to 1080p at 30fps. Adequate for everyday videos and social media content.

### Front Camera
The 16MP selfie camera is a highlight at this price. Photos come out sharp and detailed, and AI Portrait Retouching adds a natural polish to skin tones without looking overdone.

The phone also supports underwater photography, AI Perfect Shot, and AI Eraser 2.0 — AI Eraser lets you remove unwanted objects from photos with one tap.

## Battery — The Biggest Selling Point

This is where the OPPO A6s 5G truly shines.

The OPPO A6s 5G packs a 6500mAh battery with 45W fast charging support. A battery this large means:

- **Light users** — Easily 2 days on a single charge
- **Heavy users** — Comfortable full day with battery to spare
- **Gaming sessions** — 3-4 hours of continuous gaming without anxiety

The 45W SUPERVOOC charging fills the battery from zero to approximately 60% in 30 minutes — fast enough for everyday use even if you forget to charge overnight.

OPPO claims the battery will maintain over 80% capacity retention after 5 years of regular use — an impressive longevity claim backed by their lab testing.

## Software — ColorOS 15

The OPPO A6s 5G ships with ColorOS 15 based on Android 15. OPPO's software is clean, fast, and packed with useful features:

- **AI LinkBoost 3.0** — Improves Wi-Fi and 5G signal stability
- **60-Month Fluency Protection** — Software optimization to keep the phone running smoothly for 5 years
- **Smart Sidebar** — Quick access to apps without leaving your current screen
- **Screen Recording, Clone Apps, Second Space** — All included out of the box

The software experience is polished and reliable. OPPO has significantly reduced bloatware in recent years.

## Water Resistance — IP64 Rated

The OPPO A6s 5G carries an IP64 rating — meaning it is protected against dust and splashing water from any direction. The phone supports IP66/IP68/IP69 ratings in some variants, meaning it can withstand continuous water jetting and submersion up to 1.5 meters for 30 minutes.

This level of water resistance is exceptional for a phone under ₹20,000.
