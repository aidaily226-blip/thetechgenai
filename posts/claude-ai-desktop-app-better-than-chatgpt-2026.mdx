## Why Claude Desktop is Better Than ChatGPT — In Simple Words

Now here is the most important part. When you install Claude on your computer, it does things that ChatGPT simply cannot do.

Let me explain each one in very simple language.

### 1. Claude Can See Your Entire Computer Screen

When you use ChatGPT on a website, it can only see what you type to it. It has no idea what else is on your screen.

Claude Desktop is different. You can allow Claude to see your screen and understand what you are looking at. This means you can say things like:

- "Help me reply to this email" — and Claude reads the email you have open

This saves you so much time. You do not have to copy and paste things into Claude. It just sees what you are working on and helps you directly.

This is called **Claude Cowork** — and it is the biggest difference from ChatGPT.

Imagine you have a messy folder on your computer with 200 files in it. You tell Claude: "Organize this folder, sort the files by type, and create a summary report."

Claude will actually do it. By itself. While you go make a cup of tea.

You come back and the folder is organized, the files are sorted, and there is a report waiting for you.

ChatGPT cannot do this. ChatGPT can only tell you how to do it yourself. Claude actually does it for you.

Other things Claude Cowork can do on your computer:

- Create presentations from your rough notes

### 3. Claude Works Inside Your Browser Too

Claude Desktop comes with a feature called **Claude in Chrome**. This means Claude can actually use your Chrome browser for you.

You can say:

And Claude will open Chrome, navigate to websites, click things, and do it all automatically. You just watch.

This is like having a personal assistant sitting at your computer doing things for you.

Claude Desktop now has **Memory** — it remembers important things about you across all your conversations. Your preferences, your work style, things you have told it before.

You do not have to keep repeating yourself. Claude remembers.
